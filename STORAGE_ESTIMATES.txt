================================================================================
RECOMMENDED CONFIGURATIONS
================================================================================

SMALL PROJECT (~100 GB - 1 TB):
  • Major cities only (1K locations)
  • OR US subset (1K stations)
  • Daily data recommended, hourly optional

MEDIUM PROJECT (~1-10 TB):
  • North America regional grid
  • OR US comprehensive coverage
  • Recent 30-50 years
  • Both daily and hourly

LARGE PROJECT (~10-50 TB):
  • Global coarse grid (1° resolution)
  • Full historical period (1940-present)
  • Both daily and hourly

MASSIVE PROJECT (~50+ TB):
  • Global fine grid (0.25° resolution)
  • Full historical period
  • Both daily and hourly
  • Multiple climate variables

================================================================================
STORAGE TIPS
================================================================================

1. Parquet format provides ~10:1 compression vs CSV
2. Partition by year for efficient queries
3. Use Snappy compression (good balance of speed/size)
4. Consider cloud storage for datasets > 10 TB
5. Budget 20% extra for backups and intermediate files
6. SSD recommended for datasets < 5 TB, HDD okay for larger
